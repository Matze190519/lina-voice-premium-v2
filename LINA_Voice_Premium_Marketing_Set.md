## 1. Design System & Brand Guidelines

### Visual Identity: "Dark Premium Tech"
*   **Core Aesthetic:** High-end, professional, trustworthy. Combines deep dark backgrounds with precise, glowing UI elements.
*   **Color Palette:**
    *   **Backgrounds:** Deep Anthracite (`#121212`) to Rich Black (`#050505`) gradients.
    *   **Glassmorphism:** White with 10-20% opacity, background blur 20px, thin white borders (`rgba(255,255,255,0.1)`).
    *   **Primary Accent:** "Electric Cyan" (`#00F0FF`) or "LINA Blue" (`#2D7FF9`) for buttons and active elements.
    *   **Secondary Accent:** Subtile Gold (`#D4AF37`) for "Premium" highlights.
    *   **Text:** Pure White (`#FFFFFF`) for headlines, Light Grey (`#E0E0E0`) for body.
*   **Typography:**
    *   **Headlines:** Bold, geometric Sans-Serif (e.g., Montserrat Bold, Inter ExtraBold). Wide tracking.
    *   **Body:** Clean, legible Sans-Serif (e.g., Roboto, Open Sans).
*   **Key Motifs:**
    *   **Voice Waveform:** Smooth, flowing, glowing lines.
    *   **Floating UI:** Glass cards showing status updates ("Incoming Call", "Confirmed").
    *   **Lighting:** Low-key, cinematic, emphasizing depth.
