## 1. Design System (v3 Update)

**Core Philosophy:** "Grounded Reality, Elevated Tech."
We moved away from purely generative/AI-looking visuals to **high-end commercial photography** styles. The base is always a realistic, gritty, or clean professional environment (workshop, practice, office). The "AI" element is introduced solely through **sharp, minimalist UI overlays** that look like functional AR or high-res screen displays, not sci-fi magic.

**Visual Rules:**
*   **Photography:** Depth of field, natural lighting, authentic textures (wood, fabric, metal).
*   **UI Elements:** Thin lines, glowing text in brand colors (Cyan/Purple), floating slightly off-screen or perfectly integrated into device screens.
*   **Lighting:** Dark, moody atmospheres with specific rim lighting to highlight the subject and the tech.
